Communicating with a Patient's Care Team in PK Messaging
If a care team has been defined for a patient in an external system, then you may be able to communicate with a member of the care team in PK Messaging when patient context is active. The Care Team screen in PatientKeeper displays the providers on the patient's care team, and allows you to exchange messages with those providers. This may include providers in the patient's current facility with immediate responsibilities for that patient, as well the patient's primary care physician or a specialist at another facility.
Patient care team data is defined in an external system, and then pulled into PatientKeeper when the Care Team screen is accessed. Each time the screen is opened within PatientKeeper, the latest care team data is displayed, so the care team is always up-to-date.
The list of care team providers shows the following details if it is available for each provider:
•PK Messaging status- On the far left, an Online status icon , Offline status icon displays. For non-PK Messaging users, an Unknown status icon displays, since the user’s presence is unknown.
•Provider's full name as it appears in the external system.
•Provider's patient-specific role as it appears in the external system. For example, Attending or Bedside Nurse.
•Provider’s Role as defined in PatientKeeper. For example, Medical Doctor or Registered Nurse.
•Provider's Specialty as defined in PatientKeeper. For example, Cardiology (note that this is only visible to PK Messaging users and is only displayed for other PatientKeeper users with a Specialty defined).
The Care Team feature is controlled by the following administration setting, and must be setup for your site and for your user by your Administrator:
Admin - Institution - Messaging - Enable Care Team Integration
To send a message to a care team member:
1. Open patient context in a conversation by sending or receiving a patient link, as described in Sending or Receiving a Patient Link in a Message.
2. In the patient header, click the Care Team button .
The Care Team screen displays, showing the list of providers for that patient.
3. Tap a provider to see the available options to communicating.
The Send Message icon is available to communicate with care team members who are also PK Messaging users (or are non-PatientKeeper users on a supported messaging client). To start a new conversation with the provider, click the Send Message icon. A conversation screen opens with the provider, and a patient link is added to the conversation. Proceed to send a message to the care team provider as described in Starting or Continuing a Conversation, starting at Step 4.
The Information icon opens the Provider Details screen to show the available details for that care team member. If there are no details available for the provider, a message displays stating this.
